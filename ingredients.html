RepairVite Ingredients

A line-by-line look at what's inside Apex Energetics RepairVite (K-60), 173.7 g powder, ~30 servings, including active components and excipients.

What's inside? A big dose of L-glutamine, plus a short list of named herbs and minerals. No hidden blend. You can see every dose, which makes it easy to judge.

Active Ingredients

Here's each active ingredient and what it does.

  • L-Glutamine (about 5 grams or more) — the main fuel for your gut lining, and the part with the most evidence behind it.
  • DGL (deglycyrrhizinated licorice) — soothes the lining, without the blood-pressure problem regular licorice has.
  • Zinc Carnosine — the best-studied piece for protecting the upper gut.
  • Slippery Elm bark — a soothing herb that coats irritated tissue.
  • Marshmallow Root — another soothing herb, used for gut comfort for a long time.
  • Chamomile — a mild calming herb. It's also the part that can trigger a ragweed allergy.
  • Quercetin — a plant compound that calms down certain immune cells.

Other Ingredients (Excipients)

What about the inactive stuff? Not much, and nothing hidden. It's a flavored powder for cool water. So the extras are flavor and texture, not pill binders. No titanium dioxide. No big load of dye. And the real doses are printed on the label. Clean and simple.

Allergens and Sensitivities

Any allergy flags? Mainly chamomile. It cross-reacts with ragweed. So if ragweed gets you, you might react here. It's a gut formula, not a food full of allergens. Still, check the current label for the flavor system. Apex changes it sometimes. If free-form glutamine bothers you, ease in. Pregnant or nursing? Skip it, because of the licorice.

Sourcing and Quality Notes

Where do you buy the real thing? Through a practitioner or a dispensary like Fullscript. Not on a store shelf. Not officially on Amazon. There are two versions: the standard K-60 here, and the stronger RepairVite-SE (K-98). Why does this matter? Amazon listings are grey-market. You can't be sure they're real or stored right. A practitioner dispensary is the safe way to buy it. A practitioner's evaluation of Apex Energetics's sourcing standards is included in this a clinician's full review of RepairVite K-60.

How Ingredients Compare to Similar Products

What else is out there? The big alternatives are Designs for Health GI Revive and Microbiome Labs MegaMucosa. There's also Apex's own stronger RepairVite-SE (K-98). Or you can build your own — buy plain glutamine, DGL, and zinc carnosine separately. The DIY route is cheaper but more work. RepairVite puts the soothing-and-mucosal mix in one scoop, with doses you can see. MegaMucosa takes a different route. The honest truth? With a real driver, any of them can work. Without one, you're mostly paying for marketing.
