What is RepairVite?

So what is it? RepairVite (K-60) is a flavored powder. You mix it in cool water, not hot tea. The star is L-glutamine, about 5 grams or more per scoop. That's the main food for the cells lining your small intestine. Along with it you get DGL (licorice with the blood-pressure part removed), zinc carnosine, slippery elm, marshmallow root, chamomile, and quercetin. No hidden 'proprietary blend.' You can see every dose, which is rare. The tub is 173.7 grams. That's about thirty servings, or roughly two weeks at the full dose. Does it cure 'leaky gut'? No. People even argue about whether 'leaky gut' is a real diagnosis. A leaky barrier you can measure, though, is real. Think of RepairVite as support, not a cure.
